# Play & Waste 🎮

Take a break without leaving your editor. **Play & Waste** brings a collection of mini games directly into VSCode — no browser, no distractions, just games.

---

## Games

### ⭕ Tic Tac Toe
The classic two-player game. Take turns placing X and O on a 3×3 grid. First to line up three wins. Scores are tracked across rounds so you can settle the rivalry properly.

### 🐍 Snake
Guide the snake around the grid, eat the food, and grow as long as you can — without hitting the walls or yourself. Speed increases as your score climbs.

- Arrow keys or **WASD** to move
- **P** to pause
- **R** to restart

### 🔢 Sudoku
Fill the 9×9 grid so every row, column, and 3×3 box contains the numbers 1–9. Choose your difficulty and race against the clock.

- **Easy** — great for a quick break
- **Medium** — needs a bit of thinking
- **Hard** — for when you really want to waste time

Tap a cell, then type a number or use the on-screen pad. Use pencil mode (✏️) to jot down notes inside cells.

### 🃏 Memory Match
A grid of face-down cards — flip two at a time to find matching pairs. Clear the board in as few moves as possible. Three grid sizes available: 4×4, 4×6, and 6×6.

---

## How to Open a Game

**From the Activity Bar**
Click the 🎮 icon in the left sidebar → expand a category → click any game.

**From the Command Palette**
Press `Cmd+Shift+P` and type **Play & Waste** to see all available commands.

**From the Game Hub**
Run **Play & Waste: Open Game Panel** to open the hub, then click any game card.

---

## Settings

Open VSCode Settings and search **Play & Waste** to customise:

| Setting | Description |
|---|---|
| Player Name | Your display name shown in the status bar |
| Enable Status Bar | Show or hide the status bar item |
